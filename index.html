ğŸ­ What is Imposter Game?

Imposter Game (also known as "Who is the Spy" or "Undercover") is a thrilling social deduction party game where players must identify the impostor among them. In this game, most players receive the same secret word (civilians), while one player gets a different but related word (the impostor).

Our online imposter game makes it easy to play anywhere - just open your browser and start! With over 1000 words across 12 diverse categories (Food, Animals, Places, Objects, Movies, Jobs, Sports, Colors, Countries, Brands, Instruments & Emotions), every game is unique and exciting. ğŸ’¡ Tips & Strategies

For Civilians:

  • Be Specific but Vague: Describe your word in a way that proves you're a civilian without giving away the exact word.
  • Watch for Inconsistencies: Pay attention to descriptions that don't quite match or seem off.
  • Build Trust: Make sure others know you're on their team through your descriptions.
  • Ask Questions: Probe other players with questions to reveal the impostor.

For Impostors:

  • Listen First: Let others speak first to understand what word the civilians have.
  • Stay General: Use vague descriptions that could apply to many words.
  • Blend In: Mirror the civilians' description style to avoid suspicion.
  • Don't Panic: Stay calm even when questioned. Confidence is key!

How many players do I need?

You can play with 3-12 players. We recommend 5-8 players for the best experience, as it provides enough complexity without making discussions too chaotic.

Can we play this game online with friends?

Yes! Simply start a video call with your friends, share your screen, and play together. Each player can take turns viewing their word privately on their device.

What if the impostor word is too obvious?

The game automatically selects related words from the same category, but some combinations might be easier or harder. This variety keeps the game interesting! You can always start a new round if needed.

Can I add my own words?

Currently, the game uses our curated word lists to ensure balanced gameplay. However, we're considering adding custom word lists in future updates. Stay tuned!

Is this game suitable for children?

Yes! The game is family-friendly with appropriate words in all categories. It's great for developing vocabulary, critical thinking, and social skills in children aged 8 and above.

How long does a typical game take?

A typical game lasts 5-15 minutes, depending on the number of players and how long the discussion phase takes. It's perfect for quick entertainment!

What's the difference between this and Mafia/Werewolf?

While both are social deduction games, Impostor Game is simpler and faster. There's no elimination, no night phase, and everyone participates throughout. Plus, it uses words instead of role-playing, making it more accessible.

Can I save my friend groups?

Yes! Click "Create New Group" to save your regular gaming groups. Your groups are stored locally in your browser for quick access next time.
